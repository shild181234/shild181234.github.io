I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Physical and Environmental Sciences at the University of Toronto. My research focuses on physical limnology, with a specific focus on winter limnology, including mixing and stratification, dissolved oxygen dynamics, and fish habitat use in seasonally ice-covered lakes.

I am broadly interested in stratified flows in reservoirs, lakes, and rivers, particularly mixing dynamics, thermal stratification, hydrodynamic processes, and their ecological implications for oxygen dynamics, phytoplankton distributions, and aquatic habitats.
