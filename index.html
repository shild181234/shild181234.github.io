About

I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Physical and Environmental Sciences at the University of Toronto. My research focuses on physical limnology, winter limnology, dissolved oxygen dynamics, lake thermal structure, and fish habitat use in seasonally ice-covered lakes.

My broader research interests include stratified flows in reservoirs, lakes, and rivers, especially mixing dynamics, thermal stratification, hydrodynamic processes, and their ecological implications for phytoplankton distributions, oxygen dynamics, and aquatic habitats.
