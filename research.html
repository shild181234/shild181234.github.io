Research

Winter Limnology

Under-ice thermal structure, inverse stratification, benthic heat release, radiatively driven convection, and seasonal transitions in dimictic lakes.

Oxygen Dynamics

Winter dissolved oxygen storage, redistribution, ecological memory, and spring-summer oxygen conditions in large ice-covered lakes.

Fish Habitat Use

Effects of winter thermal gradients and oxygen conditions on the depth distribution and habitat use of cold-water fish.

Reservoir Hydrodynamics

Hydro-thermal processes, stratified flows, confluence mixing, turbulence, and phytoplankton transport in large channel-type reservoirs.

Stratified Flows

Gravity currents, internal waves, vertical mixing, and their roles in transporting heat, oxygen, sediment, and ecological materials.
